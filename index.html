Lift

Lift is a chrome extension that allows users to rapidly traverse to any actionable item on long webpages, such as infinite scrolling Reddit. Gives users the power to set markers of notable materials during a single browsing session and come back later.

  • Date: May 2015
  • Discipline: Web Development

ggLookup

Graphical glookup (aka. ggLookup) is a webapp designed to streamline and beautify gLookup, Berkeley EECS's command line utility tool used to check grades. ggLookup allows users to directly view their assignments and grades without having to SSH into Berkeley's server and offers more details than traditional glookup.

  • Date: Oct 2014
  • Discipline: Web Development

Polylactide

Conducted a study on the effect of synthesis environment on the glass transition temperature of PLA (polylactide), a form of biodegradable plastic. Glass transition temperature is the critical temperature at which the plastic loses its structural integral, and by increasing it, PLA could potentially become a market-competitive green material for everyday use.

  • Chemical Analysis Laboratory @ TJHSST: Paper found here
  • Date: Dec - Jun 2014
  • Discipline: Material Science

Space Invader

Classic Space Invader game re-animated on the PC using Java graphics. Featuring 3 distinct modes and a highscore system.

  • Date: April 2012
