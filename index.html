Lift

Lift is a chrome extension that allows users to rapidly traverse to any actionable item on long webpages, such as infinite scrolling Reddit. Gives users the power to set markers of notable materials during a single browsing session and come back later. Now you can download it here

  • Date: May 2015

ggLookup

Graphical glookup (aka. ggLookup) is a webapp designed to streamline and beautify gLookup, Berkeley EECS's command line utility tool used to check grades. ggLookup allows users to directly view their assignments and grades without having to SSH into Berkeley's server and offers more details than traditional glookup.

  • Date: Oct 2014
  • Discipline: Web Development

CalDining Nutrition Tracker

We were contacted by CalDining to create a web portal that allows students to find out how much nutrition they have consumed. The CalDining Nutrition Tracker allows students to register an account, record their daily diet, and visualize their dietary habits. No more worrying about "Freshman 15"!

SmartPal

    SmartPal is an android mobile and wear application that focuses on helping parents better understand their kids. Children often have trouble communicating with their parents under stress and resort to sharing their genuine emotions with stuffed animals or toys. SmartPal seeks to recreate that experience for children while allowing parents to make more informed parental choices.
